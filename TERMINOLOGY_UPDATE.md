# Terminology Clarification

**Date:** October 3, 2025

---

## Important Distinction

### Partnership vs Integration

**Partnership** - Business relationships with companies collaborating with Eagle Finance
- Use in: `/partner` section
- Examples: Business partners, strategic collaborators, institutional partners

**Integration** - Technical integration with other protocols
- Use when referring to: Charm Finance, LayerZero, Uniswap
- Examples: Protocol integrations, technical integrations, smart contract integrations

## Correct Usage

**Right:**
- "Eagle OVault integrates with Charm Finance Alpha Vaults"
- "Through our integration with Charm Finance"
- "Charm Finance integration enables..."

**Wrong:**
- "Strategic partnership with Charm Finance"
- "Our partnership with Charm Finance"

**Exception - Still use "partnership":**
- When referring to the `/partner` section for businesses
- When discussing strategic business relationships
