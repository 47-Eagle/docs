# Understanding Omnichain Vaults

Learn how 47 Eagle Finance's omnichain vault technology works and why it's revolutionary for DeFi users.

## The Problem with Traditional Vaults

Traditional DeFi vaults have significant limitations:

- **Single Chain Only** - You can only deposit and withdraw on one blockchain
- **Fragmented Liquidity** - Each chain has separate vaults with different yields
- **Complex Bridging** - Moving assets between chains requires multiple transactions
- **High Costs** - Gas fees and bridge fees eat into your returns

## How 47 Eagle Finance Solves This

### True Omnichain Experience

47 Eagle Finance creates a **single vault that exists across all supported blockchains**:

- Deposit WLFI on BSC, withdraw on Arbitrum ✅
- All deposits work together in one unified strategy ✅
- Automatic cross-chain messaging handles everything ✅
- No manual bridging or complex transactions ✅

### The Magic Behind It

#### Hub and Spoke Architecture

Think of 47 Eagle Finance like a hub-and-spoke airline system:

- **Hub (Ethereum)** - The main vault where your assets actually work
- **Spokes (All Other Chains)** - Entry and exit points you can use
- **Seamless Connections** - LayerZero handles all the cross-chain coordination

#### Your Assets' Journey

When you make a deposit:

1. **You deposit** WLFI on BSC
2. **LayerZero bridges** your WLFI to Ethereum automatically  
3. **The vault deposits** your assets into Uniswap V3 strategies
4. **You receive shares** that represent your portion of the vault
5. **Your shares** can move to any supported blockchain instantly

### What Makes This Special

#### Unified Liquidity Pool

Instead of having separate $1M vaults on 5 chains, 47 Eagle Finance creates one $5M vault. This means:

#### Instant Cross-Chain Access

Your vault shares are **Omnichain Fungible Tokens (OFTs)**:

- Move between any supported blockchain instantly
- Use as collateral on any chain
- Trade on DEXs across the ecosystem
- Always represent the same underlying value
