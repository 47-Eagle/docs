## What is Eagle Finance?

Eagle Finance brings professional liquidity management to everyone. We use **Charm Finance Alpha Vaults** to optimize your positions on Uniswap V3, while **LayerZero V2** lets you deposit and withdraw from any blockchain.

**The result:** Maximized trading fees, professional rebalancing, true cross-chain freedom.
