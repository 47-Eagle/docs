## Depositing

### On the Same Chain as Vault (Ethereum)

1. **Connect wallet** to Ethereum
2. **Enter amount** of WLFI/USD1 to deposit
3. **Approve tokens** (if first time)
4. **Submit deposit** transaction
5. **Receive vault shares** immediately

Gas cost: ~0.01-0.03 ETH

### From Different Chains (Cross-Chain Deposit)

1. **Connect wallet** on your source chain (e.g., Arbitrum)
2. **Enter amount** to deposit
3. **Approve tokens** (if first time)
4. **Submit cross-chain deposit** (includes LayerZero fee)
5. **Wait 1-5 minutes** for bridging
6. **Receive vault shares** on destination chain

Gas cost: Source chain gas + LayerZero messaging fee (~$1-5)

:::info How Cross-Chain Works
Your tokens bridge to Ethereum via LayerZero V2, deposit into the vault, then your shares bridge back to your chosen chain â€” all in one transaction.
:::

## Understanding Vault Shares

**Vault shares = Ownership receipts**

When you deposit $1,000:
- You get vault shares (e.g., 950 shares)
- Shares represent your portion of the vault
- As vault earns fees, each share becomes worth more
- When you withdraw, you burn shares and get back tokens + earnings

**Example:**
- Day 1: Deposit $1,000 â†’ Get 1,000 shares
- Day 30: Vault earns 10% â†’ Your shares now worth $1,100
- Withdraw: Burn 1,000 shares â†’ Get $1,100 back

**Shares are omnichain:**
- Work on every supported chain
- Move freely via LayerZero
- Same value everywhere

## Withdrawing

### To the Same Chain

1. **Enter amount** of shares to withdraw
2. **Submit withdraw** transaction
3. **Receive tokens** (WLFI, USD1, or both) + earnings

Processing: Instant  
Gas cost: ~0.01-0.02 ETH

### To a Different Chain

1. **Choose destination chain**
2. **Enter amount** of shares to withdraw
3. **Submit cross-chain withdraw** (includes LayerZero fee)
4. **Wait 1-5 minutes** for bridging
5. **Receive tokens** on destination chain

Processing: 1-5 minutes  
Gas cost: Source chain gas + LayerZero messaging fee (~$1-5)

:::tip Withdrawal Fees
- **Small withdrawals** (less than 1% of vault): No fees
- **Large withdrawals** (more than 10% of vault): ~0.1-0.5% fee to cover liquidity removal costs
- Fees go back into the vault (benefit remaining depositors)
:::

## Security Best Practices

- **Verify contract addresses** before approving
- **Start with small deposits** to test the process
- **Keep seed phrases safe** and never share them
- **Use hardware wallets** for large amounts
- **Check transaction details** before signing
- **Bookmark official app** â€” avoid phishing sites
