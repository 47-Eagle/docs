# Privacy Policy

*Last Updated: [Date]*

## 1. Introduction

47 Eagle Finance, Inc. ("47 Eagle Finance," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our Eagle Omnichain Vault Protocol and related services.

By using our services, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We may collect the following types of personal information:

**Identity and Contact Information:**
- Full name and legal name
- Email address and phone number
- Residential and business addresses
- Date of birth and nationality
- Government-issued identification documents

**Financial Information:**
- Bank account and payment information
- Income and net worth verification
- Investment experience and sophistication
- Source of funds documentation
- Tax identification numbers

**Technical Information:**
- Wallet addresses and transaction history
- Device information and IP addresses
- Browser type and operating system
- Usage patterns and interaction data
- Cookies and tracking technologies

### Blockchain Data
As a DeFi protocol, we necessarily interact with public blockchain networks where:
- Transaction data is publicly available
- Wallet addresses may be linked to your identity
- On-chain activity is permanently recorded
- Smart contract interactions are transparent

### Security Measures
We implement comprehensive security measures including:

**Technical Safeguards:**
- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Multi-factor authentication requirements
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Secure development and deployment practices
- Network monitoring and intrusion detection

**Organizational Controls:**

- Access controls and need-to-know principles
- Incident response and breach procedures

Users should understand that:

- On-chain data is publicly visible

## 7. Data Retention

### Retention Periods
We retain personal information for:
- **Active users**: Duration of relationship plus 7 years
- **KYC/AML data**: As required by applicable law (typically 5-7 years)
- **Transaction records**: Permanent retention for blockchain compliance
- **Marketing data**: Until consent withdrawal or 3 years of inactivity

### Secure Deletion
When retention periods expire, we securely delete information using:
- Cryptographic erasure methods
- Multi-pass data wiping
- Physical destruction of storage media
- Certificate of destruction documentation
